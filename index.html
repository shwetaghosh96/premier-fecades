About Us

Established in 2012, Premier Facades brings together over 70 years of experience in the facades industry to deliver a variety of skills including curtain walling, specialised renders, cladding and steel framing systems. With capability to handle a full building envelope as well as individual elements within a broader group of specialists, our experienced team and project management skills ensure a quality finish.
When using Premier Facades, you get the benefit of working with a multi-disciplined company with decades of knowledge of the facades industry, coupled with a high level of personal service.
Our business has grown because of returning and referred clients – in fact, we’ve never lost a client – and our experienced estimators ensure that budgets only change if scope does.
